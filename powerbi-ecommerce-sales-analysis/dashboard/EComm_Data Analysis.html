🏆 Technology: Star Performer
Technology drives $4.74M (37.5%) of revenue with $663K profit. Phones and Copiers alone account for $3.2M. Highest margin category.
⚠ Tables: Cash Drain
$757K revenue but -$64K profit. Every sale loses money. Driven by excessive discounting. Urgent review needed.
🌏 APAC: Growth Engine
Leads all 7 markets at $3.58M revenue and $436K profit (12.2% margin). Central region is #1 globally at $2.82M.
💸 Discounts Destroying Profit
Discounts above 30% generate -$381K net loss. Cap at 20% would recover this entirely. #1 profit lever available.
📅 Strong Q4 Seasonality
Nov–Dec consistently 30–40% above average. September also peaks. Pre-stock inventory by August to avoid stockouts.
⚡ SE Asia: Margin Risk
$884K revenue but only $17.8K profit (2.0%). Lowest of all regions. Likely heavy discounting or shipping cost overrun.
📈 4-Year Revenue CAGR
Revenue grew from $2.26M to $4.30M — a 17.4% CAGR. 2013→2014 was the strongest year (+26.3%). Momentum accelerating.
🚚 Shipping: Hidden Cost Driver
Total shipping cost ~$1.35M — nearly equal to total profit. Standard Class (59.9% of orders) is the dominant mode. Premium modes underutilised.
👥 Consumer Segment Dominates
Consumer = 51.5% of revenue ($6.5M). Corporate at 30.2% is the biggest B2B growth opportunity with higher average order values.
